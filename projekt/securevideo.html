securevideo

Category: security — rust, python

Status: WIP

Overview

SecureVideo is a hardware-backed backup tool for offloading large video and photo files from a camera or SD card. Running on a LattePanda IOTA single-board computer, it copies the source to two separate SSDs at once and verifies via checksum that both copies are uncorrupted and byte-identical — so footage can’t be silently lost. A collaboration with my brother for his studies at ETH Zürich.

Features

  • runs on a LattePanda IOTA single-board computer
  • simultaneous copy to two separate SSDs (redundancy)
  • checksum verification of every copy against the source
  • planned: an Arduino status display, and a possible rewrite in C
